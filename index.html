Pig Dice

Instructions on how to play the game:

During each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a dice untile he/she rolls a 1 or decides to "hold".

  1. If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the other player's turn.
  2. If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues.
  3. If the player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their score and it is the other player's turn.

The first player to score 100 or more points wins!
